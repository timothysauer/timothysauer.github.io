\(\def\ds{\displaystyle} \) 1 (a) Euler's Method generates \(w_{i1}\) and \(w_{i2}, i=1, 2, 3, 4\), to approximate \(y_1\) and \(y_2\), respectively. For step size \(h=1/4\), the formulas are \[w_{i+1,1} = w_{i1}+h(w_{i1}+w_{i2})\ \ \ \ \text{and}\ \ \ \ w_{i+1,2} = w_{i2}+h(-w_{i1}+w_{i2}).\] Starting with the initial conditions \(w_{01}=y_1(0)=1\) and \(w_{02}=y_2(0)=0\), the approximate solutions are \[\begin{array}{ll} w_{01}=1&w_{02}=0\\ \\ w_{11}=1+\frac{1}{4}(1+0) = \frac{5}{4}&w_{12}=0+\frac{1}{4}(-1+0) = -\frac{1}{4}\\ \\ w_{21}=\frac{5}{4}+\frac{1}{4}(\frac{5}{4}-\frac{1}{4}) = \frac{3}{2}& w_{22}=-\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{4}(-\frac{5}{4}-\frac{1}{4})= -\frac{5}{8}\\ \\ w_{31}=\frac{3}{2}+\frac{1}{4}(\frac{3}{2}-\frac{5}{8}) = \frac{55}{32}& w_{32}=-\frac{5}{8}+\frac{1}{4}(-\frac{3}{2}-\frac{5}{8})= -\frac{37}{32}\\ \\ w_{41}=\frac{55}{32}+\frac{1}{4}(\frac{55}{32}-\frac{37}{32}) = \frac{119}{64}\ \ \ \ \ & w_{42}=-\frac{37}{32}+\frac{1}{4}(-\frac{55}{32}-\frac{37}{32})= -\frac{15}{8} \\ \end{array}\] The correct solutions can be used to find the global truncation errors of the approximations. The global error of \(w_{41}\) is \(|y_1(1)-w_{41}| \approx 0.3907\). The global error of \(w_{42}\) is \(|y_2(1)-w_{42}| \approx 0.4124\).
